Education Program

Empowering minds through access to quality education and resources.

What We Do

At Break the Chain of Poverty, our Education Program is committed to unlocking opportunities for students in underserved communities. We provide essential resources like books, school supplies, and uniforms, and we strive to empower the next generation by working alongside local educators and families.

We help students stay in school, grow in confidence, and pursue their dreams through education.

Our Focus Areas

  • Providing school supplies to children in need
  • Supporting literacy and reading clubs
  • Training passionate teachers and volunteers
  • Building and renovating safe, inclusive classrooms

Schools

We have 7 American Academy of Excellence schools in different areas of Kinshasa, serving over 6,000 students.
